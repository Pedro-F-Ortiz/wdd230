Down Town Conroe

Many wonderful attractions in the downtown of old Conroe with plenty of restaurants, pubs, classic theatre. Enjoy a wonderful time out with the family any day of the week or weekend events.

Lake side golf course

You love to golf? Enjoy a wonderful summer day or evening in our lake side 20 hole golf course. With stunning views and beautiful Texas weather, good memories await.

HEB Texas Chain grocery stores

Need groceries? Look no further, visit one of the many location's of the Texas local grocery stores HEB where you are sure to find a bargain and fair priced items.

Houston Texas Medical Center

Fear not for your health, Houston is just a hop away and in the heart of its downtown you will find a medical center with all the major hospitals. Conroe itself with 3 hospital branches ready to host patients in need.

Minute Maid Baseball Park

Minute Maid Park is the home of the Houston Astros baseball team ready to host games in their stadium. With a retractable roof, enjoy a nice cool day and beautiful summer skies or closed for the winter or rainy days.

TopGolf

Visit Conroe's newest outdoor golf driving range at TopGolf where family fun awaits.

Conroe's nature Trails

Enjoy one of many nature trails with ponds or lake side views that conroe has to offer. Wild life is sure to be spotted year round in any of the nature trails Conroe has to offer.

Community Parks

Conroe is home to many parks within the community or public parks ready to provide families with safe gathering pavilions to host outdoor parties and family friendly events.

Typhoon Water Park

Summer time in Texas gets really hot so come enjoy a refreshing day at Conroe's finest water park, Typhoon Texas.
