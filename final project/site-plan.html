Overview

Site Purpose

The purpose for my website is to promote my sister's local restaurant to increase sales. My sister owns a Mexican restaurant that is small so she doesn't have a website or a way for clients to know the menu.

Target Audience

The audience is going to be customer's that are looking for good Mexican food. They will be able to see an online menu with current prices and an image of the plate of food for customers that do not know what the plate of food is. It will also include a section with ingredients so customers know what toppings come with the plate or what they would like to take out.
